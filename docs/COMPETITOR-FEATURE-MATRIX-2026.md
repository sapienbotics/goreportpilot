## Part 2: Per-Competitor Deep Dive

### AgencyAnalytics — Threat Level: **HIGH**

**What they do best:** The most complete all-in-one agency reporting platform. 85+ integrations, polished drag-and-drop builder, client portals, white-label, and a growing AI feature set. Market leader in the agency reporting space with a strong brand.

**What they lack (our opportunity):** No PPTX export. AI features are locked to higher tiers ($179+) and produce only short widget-level summaries, not full narrative reports. Per-client surcharges ($20/client) make it expensive at scale — an agency with 30 clients pays $579/month. No multi-language AI. No tone control on AI output.

**Their ideal customer vs ours:** They target established agencies (10-50 clients) willing to pay $179-$500+/month for a dashboard-first platform. We target cost-sensitive freelancers and small agencies who need presentation-ready reports (PPTX + AI narrative) at a fraction of the cost.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** Poor for small agencies. $59/month for 5 clients with limited AI. GoReportPilot offers more AI depth at $19/month.

**Sources:** [AgencyAnalytics Pricing](https://agencyanalytics.com/pricing) · [AgencyAnalytics AI](https://agencyanalytics.com/features/ai-reporting-tools)

---

### DashThis — Threat Level: **MEDIUM**

**What they do best:** Simple, beautiful dashboards. 34+ integrations covering core marketing channels. The new source-based pricing (March 2026) is more transparent than before. AI Insights Pro add-on with tone control and context is genuinely useful.

**What they lack:** No PPTX export. No email delivery with attachments (dashboards only). AI is a paid add-on ($19/month) even on the $139/month plan. Dashboard-first (not report-first), which means no structured multi-page report output. The source-based pricing can get expensive with many data accounts.

**Their ideal customer:** Agencies that want live dashboards for clients to log into, not polished PDF/PPTX reports. We target agencies that present reports in meetings and need editable slides.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** Moderate. $44/month for 3 dashboards is reasonable, but $139/month for 10 dashboards + $19 AI = $158 total vs our $39 for 15 clients with AI included.

**Sources:** [DashThis Pricing](https://dashthis.com/pricing/) · [DashThis AI Insights](https://dashthis.com/ai-insights/)

---

### Whatagraph — Threat Level: **LOW**

**What they do best:** Data blending across sources, advanced data warehouse export (BigQuery), and strong visualization. Good for data-heavy agencies that need to combine multiple data streams. Recently added Whatagraph IQ (AI chatbot).

**What they lack:** No PPTX export. Requires annual billing only (no monthly option). No free trial on paid plans. Starting at ~$229/month, it's priced for established agencies. AI features are basic (chatbot + short summaries). Users on G2 complain about pricing and limited value for smaller agencies.

**Their ideal customer:** Mid-size agencies (20-50 clients) with data warehouse needs. Not a match for freelancers or cost-sensitive agencies.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** Poor for small teams. $229/month minimum with annual commitment is 6x our Pro plan.

**Sources:** [Whatagraph Pricing](https://whatagraph.com/pricing) · [Whatagraph G2 Reviews](https://www.g2.com/products/whatagraph/reviews)

---

### Swydo — Threat Level: **LOW-MEDIUM**

**What they do best:** Clean, professional reports with full white-labeling at all price levels. 32+ integrations with strong PPC platform coverage (including TikTok Ads). All features included at base price — no feature gating. Data blending from up to 5 platforms per widget.

**What they lack:** No AI features whatsoever. No PPTX export (PDF only). No CSV/Excel export. Per-source pricing escalates quickly — 100 sources = $474/month. No client portal.

**Their ideal customer:** PPC agencies that need simple, branded PDF reports and value transparent pricing. We win on AI narrative and PPTX.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** Good for small accounts (10 sources = $69), poor at scale.

**Sources:** [Swydo Pricing](https://www.swydo.com/pricing/) · [Swydo Review](https://whatagraph.com/reviews/swydo)

---

### Databox — Threat Level: **MEDIUM**

**What they do best:** 100+ integrations, strong goal tracking, and genuinely good AI summaries (powered by OpenAI). The AI generates plain-language summaries with adjustable length, style, and multi-language support. Good for agencies that want data-driven goal monitoring.

**What they lack:** No PPTX export. White-labeling costs $250/month extra. AI is locked to Growth ($399/month) and Premium ($799/month) plans. The $79 agency Essential plan has no AI. No scheduled PDF delivery on lower tiers.

**Their ideal customer:** Data-driven agencies focused on goal tracking and KPI monitoring. Not optimized for client-facing report delivery.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** AI features at $399/month vs our $19/month with AI included. Databox's AI is more mature but 20x the price.

**Sources:** [Databox Agency Pricing](https://databox.com/pricing-agency) · [Databox AI](https://help.databox.com/overview-databoxs-generative-ai)

---

### Reporting Ninja — Threat Level: **MEDIUM** (on price)

**What they do best:** Cheapest full-featured reporting tool at $20/month. Full white-labeling on all plans. Transparent pricing unchanged since 2015. Google Sheets + Looker Studio export included. No upsells or feature gating.

**What they lack:** No AI features at all. No PPTX export. Limited to ~20 integrations. No client portal. Basic chart/visualization options. Dated UI compared to modern competitors.

**Their ideal customer:** Budget-conscious freelancers who need basic automated reports. They compete with us on price but lose on AI and PPTX.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** Excellent for basic needs. $20/month for all features is hard to beat. But no AI means no narrative commentary — users still need to write insights manually.

**Sources:** [Reporting Ninja Pricing](https://www.reportingninja.com/pricing) · [Reporting Ninja Integrations](https://www.reportingninja.com/integrations)

---

### NinjaCat — Threat Level: **LOW** (different market)

**What they do best:** Enterprise-grade reporting with 750+ integrations, PPTX export, AI Agents for campaign automation, and full white-label. The only direct competitor that matches our AI + PPTX combination.

**What they lack:** No self-serve signup. Enterprise pricing ($3,000+ contracts). Implementation costs $500-$10,000. 8-12 week deployment timeline. Completely inaccessible to freelancers and small agencies.

**Their ideal customer:** Large agencies (50+ clients) and enterprises. Not a competitive threat to GoReportPilot's target market.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** N/A — different market entirely. But this validates that AI + PPTX is an enterprise-valued combination we're offering at 1/50th the price.

**Sources:** [NinjaCat G2](https://www.g2.com/products/ninjacat/pricing) · [NinjaCat AI](https://docs.ninjacat.io/docs/using-the-text-heading-ai-insights-generator-widgets)

### Supermetrics — Threat Level: **LOW** (different category)

**What they do best:** Premier data pipeline tool. 100+ connectors. Excellent for pulling data into Google Sheets, Looker Studio, BigQuery, or Excel. Recently added Claude/ChatGPT as "destinations" for AI analysis.

**What they lack:** Not a reporting tool — it's an ETL/data connector. No native report generation. No PDF/PPTX export. No dashboards. No scheduling. Requires a separate visualization tool (Looker Studio, Power BI, etc.). Annual billing only.

**Their ideal customer:** Data analysts who build custom reports in BI tools. Not a direct competitor to GoReportPilot's automated report generation.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** N/A — different product category. But we should note that some users combine Supermetrics + Looker Studio as a DIY alternative to dedicated reporting tools.

**Sources:** [Supermetrics Pricing](https://supermetrics.com/pricing)

### Oviond — Threat Level: **MEDIUM**

**What they do best:** 50+ integrations with AI-powered insights (BYOK OpenAI on Professional+). Clean per-client pricing that scales smoothly. Good automated reporting with templates. Competitive pricing starting at $39/month for 3 clients.

**What they lack:** No PPTX export. AI requires bringing your own OpenAI key (Professional+ plans). Limited customization compared to AgencyAnalytics. Smaller market presence.

**Their ideal customer:** Small-to-mid agencies wanting a balance of features and price. Closest to our positioning but without PPTX and with less sophisticated AI.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** Good. $39/month for 3 clients with AI is similar to our Pro plan, but our Pro serves 15 clients with AI included (no BYOK required).

**Sources:** [Oviond Pricing](https://www.oviond.com/pricing/) · [Oviond G2](https://www.g2.com/products/oviond/reviews)

### Klipfolio / PowerMetrics — Threat Level: **LOW**

**What they do best:** Metric-centric analytics platform with AI-powered natural language queries. Deep data warehouse integrations (Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery). Good for internal analytics teams.

**What they lack:** Not designed for client reporting. No PDF/PPTX export. No report scheduling. No white-labeling. No client management. More of an analytics/BI tool than a reporting tool.

**Their ideal customer:** Internal data teams, not agencies. Not a direct competitor.

**Sources:** [Klipfolio PowerMetrics](https://www.klipfolio.com/powermetrics) · [PowerMetrics AI](https://www.klipfolio.com/blog/powermetrics-ai)

### Metrics Watch — Threat Level: **LOW**

**What they lack:** No PDF export. No PPTX export. No dashboards. No AI features. Very limited at $49/month (only 2 reports). Pricing scales steeply ($149 for 25 reports, $399 for 100).

**Their ideal customer:** Agencies wanting email-only report delivery. Not a feature competitor to GoReportPilot.

**Sources:** [Metrics Watch Pricing](https://metricswatch.com/pricing/)

### Octoboard — Threat Level: **LOW-MEDIUM** (on price)

**What they do best:** Cheapest per-client pricing ($5/client/month). 50+ integrations. Full white-labeling included. Client portals. Uses ChatGPT/OpenAI for automated trend analysis. 100+ pre-built templates.

**What they lack:** No PPTX export. AI features are basic (trend detection, not narrative generation). No tone control. UI is dated. Limited report customization compared to drag-and-drop builders.

**Their ideal customer:** Budget-conscious agencies that want automated dashboards + reports at minimal per-client cost. They compete on price but not on AI depth or PPTX.

**Price-to-feature ratio:** Excellent at low volumes. 10 clients = $50/month with white-labeling. But no AI narrative and no PPTX.

**Sources:** [Octoboard](https://www.octoboard.com/agencies)
